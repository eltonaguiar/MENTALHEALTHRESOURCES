  • ‚úì Reach out to someone you trust - friend, family, or professional
  • ‚úì Call or text a crisis line - trained counselors are ready to listen
  • ‚úì Go to your nearest emergency room if you're in danger
  • ‚úì Call 911 if you're having thoughts of harming yourself or others

You Are Not Alone

If you're struggling with mental health, know that:

  • Millions of people experience mental health challenges
  • Professional help is available and highly effective
  • Recovery is possible with time, support, and effort
  • Reaching out is a sign of strength, not weakness
  • You deserve support and compassion (including from yourself)
