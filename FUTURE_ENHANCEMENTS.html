Guardrailed assistant that detects crisis language and immediately escalates to on-page crisis CTAs (9-8-8, 911, Kids Help Phone) before offering any non-crisis tips.

  • Keyword and intent detection for self-harm, harm-to-others, and medical emergencies
  • Locks responses to short, directive steps with clear crisis phone/text buttons
  • Disables non-crisis suggestions when risk is detected
  • Shows safety disclaimer and privacy notice with every crisis-triggered reply

  • Privacy-first: no account required; no PII persisted by default

Contextual Search & Filters HIGH PRIORITY

Site-wide search that surfaces resources by topic, modality, language, province, and cost, with evidence/type badges.
