# Mental Health Resources for Canada

## Crisis & Immediate Support

### National Crisis Helplines
- **Canada Suicide Prevention Service**: 1-833-456-4566 (24/7, available across Canada)
- **Crisis Text Line**: Text HOME to 741741 (available 24/7)
- **Talk Suicide Canada**: 1-833-456-4566
- **Kids Help Phone**: 1-800-668-6868 (for youth under 20)
- **Healthy Minds Canada**: 1-855-838-4357 (mental health support)

### Provincial Crisis Lines
- **Alberta**: 780-424-2424
- **British Columbia**: 1-800-784-2433
- **Manitoba**: 1-877-766-0111
- **Newfoundland & Labrador**: 1-888-737-4668
- **Nova Scotia**: 1-902-429-8167
- **Ontario**: 1-800-463-2338
- **Quebec**: 1-866-826-3311
- **Saskatchewan**: 1-833-SASKTEL

## Government Resources

- **Government of Canada Mental Health Services**: [canada.ca/mental-health](https://www.canada.ca/en/health-canada/services/health-concerns/mental-health-substance-use.html)
- **Canadian Mental Health Association (CMHA)**: [cmha.ca](https://cmha.ca) - Local chapters across Canada offering support, education, and advocacy
- **Mental Health & Substance Use Services**: Provincial health ministry websites

## Mental Health Organizations

- **Anxiety Canada**: [anxietycanada.com](https://www.anxietycanada.com) - Free downloadable resources for anxiety management
- **CAMH (Centre for Addiction and Mental Health)**: [camh.ca](https://www.camh.ca) - Toronto-based leader in mental health research and care
- **Mood Disorders Society of Canada**: [mdsc.ca](https://www.mdsc.ca) - Support for mood disorders
- **Schizophrenia Canada**: [schizophreniacanada.ca](https://www.schizophreniacanada.ca)
- **Canada Mental Health Council**: Mental health advocacy and resources

## Therapy & Counseling Services

- **Psychology Today Canada Directory**: Find licensed therapists by province
- **Telehealth Counseling Services**:
  - Telus Health MindBeacon
  - Virtual care through provincial health plans
- **Employee Assistance Programs (EAP)**: Often provided through employers (typically 3-6 free counseling sessions)
- **Community Mental Health Centers**: Available in most cities and regions

## Substance Use Support

- **Narcotics Anonymous Canada**: [na.org](https://www.na.org)
- **Alcoholics Anonymous Canada**: [aa.org](https://www.aa.org)
- **Addictions and Mental Health Services**: Provincial government services

## LGBTQ+ Support

- **The 519**: [the519.org](https://the519.org) - Toronto-based, but offers resources for LGBTQ+ individuals across Canada
- **Trans Lifeline**: 1-877-565-8488 (support specifically for transgender individuals)
- **Rainbow Health Ontario**: [rainbowhealthontario.ca](https://www.rainbowhealthontario.ca)

## Self-Help & Online Resources

- **Anxiety Canada Self-Help Guides**: Free downloadable PDFs
- **Cognitive Behavioral Therapy (CBT) Apps**: MindShift (Anxiety Canada), MindSciences, Moodpath
- **Meditation & Mindfulness**: Headspace, Calm, Insight Timer (many offer Canadian resources)
- **Mental Health Blogs & Podcasts**: Various Canadian organizations offer educational content

## Prevention & Wellness

- **Mental Health America**: [mhanational.org](https://www.mhanational.org) - Screening tools and resources
- **Workplace Mental Health**: Many Canadian organizations offer wellness programs
- **Peer Support Groups**: Available through community centers and online communities

## Financial & Legal Support

- **Disability Tax Credit (DTC)**: Government support for mental health disabilities
- **Registered Disability Savings Plans (RDSPs)**: Available through Canadian banks
- **Legal Aid Services**: Many provinces offer legal support for mental health-related issues

---

**Note**: If you're in crisis or having thoughts of suicide, please contact a crisis line immediately. You don't have to face this alone.
